# Hardware examples (reference only)

These are **real-world hardware examples** used for OnPremise SRT Server deployments.
They are **not strict requirements**. Final capacity depends on total bitrate, packet loss, RTT, and network conditions.

---

## DELL PowerEdge R240 (1U rack server)

- CPU: Intel® Xeon® E-2234 3.6GHz, 8M cache, 4C/8T, turbo (71W)
- RAM: 16GB 2666MT/s DDR4 ECC UDIMM
- Price: ~870 €

Notes:
- Commonly used as a 24/7 datacenter server
- 256+ streams (reference)
- ~1 Gbps total streaming capacity (reference)
- Suitable for cable/satellite/terrestrial headends

---

## HPE ProLiant DL20 Gen10 (1U rack server)

- CPU: Intel® Pentium® G5420 (2 cores/4 threads, 3.8 GHz)
- RAM: 8 GB UDIMM
- Price: ~580 €

Notes:
- Commonly used as a 24/7 datacenter server
- 128+ streams (reference)
- ~600 Mbps total streaming capacity (reference)
- Suitable for headends or small studios

---

## Minisforum Z83-F (fanless)

- CPU: Intel Atom X5-Z8350 (up to 1.92 GHz)
- RAM: 4 GB DDR3L
- Price: ~130 €

Notes:
- Completely silent operation
- ~20 inputs and/or ~20 outputs (reference)
- ~200 Mbps max streaming capacity (reference)
- Suitable for small studios or system testing
